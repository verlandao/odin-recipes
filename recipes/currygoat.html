Curry Goat

  • 3 ½ pounds baby goat meat, cut into small cubes
  • salt to taste
  • 3 teaspoons ground turmeric, divided
  • 5 tablespoons vegetable oil
    • <1i>teaspoon white sugar
  • 3 medium potatoes, halved
    • 3 pods cardamom
  • 4 whole cloves
  • 1 (1 inch) piece cinnamon stick
  • 3 medium onions, thinly sliced
  • green chile peppers, minced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (1 inch) piece minced fresh ginger root
  • 2 medium tomatoes, sliced
  • ¾ teaspoon chili powder
  • 2 ¼ cups water, divided, or as needed
  • freshly chopped cilantro

Steps

  1. Boil the meat in the pot for 30 minutes with spices
  2. Stir fry garlic, onions chilli peppers until golden brown
  3. Add tomatos and keep stew until combined
  4. Add to the boiled meat and stew for another 20 minutes
  5. Enjoy
