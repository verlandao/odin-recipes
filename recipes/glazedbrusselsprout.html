Glazed Brussel Spouts

  • 8 slices bison bacon
  • 2 pounds Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil, or as needed
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
    • l
  • ½ cup balsamic vinegar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon black pepper

Steps

  1. Clean and cut each sprout in half
  2. In a pan add butter then add sprouts
  3. Add garlic, balsamic vinegar, salt and pepper
  4. Cook until fork tender
  5. Cook bacon until crispy in another pan
  6. Add all together and enjoy
