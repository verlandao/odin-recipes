Lasagna

  • 8 ounces lasagna noodles, broken in half
  • 1 (24-ounce) jar tomato sauce
  • 8 ounces shredded whole milk mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Boil noodles
  2. Layer cooked noodles in baking pan
  3. Layer some of the mozzarella cheese
  4. Layer some sauce
  5. Repeat 3 times
  6. Bake for 30 minutes
  7. Enjoy
